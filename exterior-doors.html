Selecting the door model starts with choice of steel base, continues with exterior and interior finish options and finishes with selection of hardware.

Steel Base

The base of exterior door is composed from a weight bearing metalic ribs and steel plates. Trapezioid profile of the ribs makes frame burglar resistant. The space between plates is filled with mineral wool for insulation and sound proofing.

FACTOR

Select FACTOR base for places with no direct atmosphere access, like the door between house and garage.

DS-7

DS-7 is fully equipped to stand the temperature drop.

DS-10

DS-10 is an upgrade from DS-7. It is thicker and features a thermal break.

Exterior Finish

You have plenty of choices: Flat metal (laminated or painted); Decorative metal (embossing, forging, add-on or aerography); Decorative panels (plastic, laminated, painted, veneered or solid wood).
